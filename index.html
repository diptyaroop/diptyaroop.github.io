About Me

I am a first-year PhD student at the College of Information and Computer Sciences (CICS) in University of Massachusetts Amherst. My research interests are broadly Distributed Systems, Sustainable Computing and Networking. I am currently working with , working with Prof. Ramesh K. Sitaraman and Prof. Prashant Shenoy on the CarbonFirst project, which aims to make edge and cloud computing sustainable and carbon-free.

Prior to this, I completed my Master's from IIT-Bombay under the supervision of Prof. Mythili Vutukuru. There, I designed and developed a high-performance User Plane Function (UPF) dataplane in 5G as part of my thesis.

Besides academics, I am really passionate about football (soccer). I follow the EPL regularly, and am up anytime for any football related discussion.
